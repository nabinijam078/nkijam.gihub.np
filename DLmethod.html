Duckworth Lewis Target Calculator

To use the score calculator follow these steps:

1. Enter the score and wickets for team A (Team A is the team batting first)

2. Enter the number of overs and balls Team B has faced (e.g. 34.3)

3. Enter the number of wickets Team B has lost so far

4. Enter the maximum number of overs each team can face

5. Click on 'UPDATE' for the winning target for Team B to be displayed.

6. You can use the +/- to increase/decrease the number of balls bowled one at a time.

NOTE: The target is the winning score. 1 less will result in a tie,
2 less in a loss.

To calculate the number of points each team scores click on
'SHOW DOCKWORTH LEWIS POINTS CALCULATOR' at the bottom.

DOCKWORTH LEWIS POINT CALCULATOR

To use the points calculator follow these steps:

1. Enter the score and wickets for team A
(Team A is the team batting first)

2. Enter the score Team B had and the wickets lost at the game interruption

3. Enter the number of overs and balls Team B has faced (e.g. 34.3)

4. Enter the maximum number of overs for the innings

5. Click on 'CALCULATE' to display the result and the number of points each team scores.

NOTE: Details of the number of wickets Team A needed to take to win or the percentage of Team A's total that had been scored by team B are shown for the losing team.
