I am Nabin Kumar Ijam. I am from Nepal and currently working in Malaysia. I am professionally a Civil Engineer. I am mainly specialized in Structural Engineering and Hydropower Engineering. During these 8 years, I have completed one hydropower project when I worked in Sanima Hydropower.
